Abstract

We introduce How2Sign, a multimodal and multiview continuous American Sign Language (ASL) dataset,
consisting of a parallel corpus of more than 80 hours of sign language videos and
a set of corresponding modalities including speech, English transcripts, and depth.
A three-hour subset was further recorded in the Panoptic studio enabling detailed 3D pose estimation.
